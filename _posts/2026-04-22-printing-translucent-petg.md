## The Beauty of Translucency
I recently experimented with Translucent PETG on the **P1S**, and the results are unique compared to standard solids. However, getting that "glass-like" look requires more than just hitting print.

### Key Takeaways:
* **Infill Matters:** Since you can see through the walls, standard grid infill can look messy. I found that **100% infill** or **concentric patterns** work best for light transmission.
* **Speed vs. Clarity:** Printing slower and slightly hotter (around 255°C) helps the layers fuse better, reducing the "frosting" effect.
* **Dry Your Filament:** PETG is a sponge for moisture. Any bubbles from wet filament show up instantly as white streaks inside the print.
